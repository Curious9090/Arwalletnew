What is AR Wallet?

AR Wallet, which people also know by the names ARB Wallet or ARB Pay, is a platform where people trade in ARB tokens. It works on a C2C (Customer-to-Customer) system. Both the buyer and seller connect their UPI apps — such as Google Pay, PhonePe, Mobikwik, Super Money, Freo, or Twid. When payment comes into your account, the system detects it automatically, ARB tokens are deducted, and the trade becomes successful.

Is AR Wallet Safe or Fake?

Yes, AR Wallet (ARB Wallet or ARB Pay) is considered safe by many users. However, you must stay careful while making payments or buying tokens. Some scammers may pretend to be AR Wallet customer support and ask for your OTP — never share your OTP or password with anyone.

Signs of a Legitimate Wallet App

  • Fast selling and smooth transactions
  • Clear terms before every transaction
  • No direct holding of user funds (C2C model)
  • Official support only through in-app live chat

Warning Signs of Scam Wallets

  • Asking for OTP or password over phone or Telegram
  • Login links sent through unknown messages
  • Websites that look like AR Wallet but have different URLs
  • Anyone claiming to be AR Wallet support contacting you first

Important: AR Wallet never contacts users first. You can test a suspicious site by entering a wrong password — if it still logs in, it is a phishing site.
