What is AR Wallet?

AR Wallet is a peer-to-peer (C2C) digital asset platform built for Indian users. It enables seamless buying and selling of ARB tokens directly between users. When you purchase 100 ARB tokens, you receive 107 tokens—7 bonus tokens credited instantly. 1 ARB token maintains a baseline value of ₹1, with market-driven price fluctuations creating earning opportunities.

How to Use AR Wallet / ARB Wallet?

AR Wallet and ARB Wallet refer to the same application. Getting started takes only two minutes: register via the download button, complete your profile, and link your UPI ID. To buy ARB, tap "Buy" on the home screen and select an offer. To sell, tap "Sell" and wait for a buyer match. Every transaction is secured by the platform's C2C confirmation system.

AR Wallet – Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is AR Wallet completely free?

Account creation and basic wallet functions are 100% free. Buying or selling ARB requires you to fund your own UPI transactions. The platform may deduct a small service fee on completed trades, which is clearly disclosed before order confirmation. There are no hidden charges.

How trustworthy is AR Wallet?

AR Wallet operates on a customer-to-customer (C2C) model—funds move directly between buyer and seller UPI accounts; the platform never holds user money. This design minimizes institutional risk. However, as with any financial activity involving other individuals, users must remain vigilant. Use only the official download link, enable all security features, and never share sensitive credentials.

What is the withdrawal process?

AR Wallet does not process bank withdrawals directly. To liquidate your holdings, you must sell your ARB tokens to another user. The buyer transfers the agreed amount to your UPI ID. Once you verify the credit in your bank account, you mark the order as complete. This peer-to-peer mechanism ensures instant settlement without withdrawal delays.

I sold ARB but didn't receive payment—what now?

If funds are not credited to your UPI-linked bank account within a reasonable time, do not press "Confirm Receipt." Immediately hit the "Appeal" button to freeze the transaction and notify AR Wallet support. Our team will manually review payment proofs and take appropriate action. Confirming receipt without actual payment is irreversible.

Can I operate multiple accounts?

No. AR Wallet enforces a strict one-user, one-account policy. Duplicate accounts created with the same mobile number, UPI ID, or device fingerprint will be detected and permanently suspended without prior notice. This policy protects the community from fraudulent activity.

How long until my sell order is matched?

Matching time depends on current buyer demand. During peak hours, orders may match instantly. During low-activity periods, you may experience longer wait times. If your order remains unmatched, you can cancel it at any time and relist later. The platform continuously works to improve liquidity.

What if a buyer sends a fake UPI screenshot?

Screenshots can be manipulated. Always verify payment by checking your actual bank balance or UPI transaction history before confirming receipt. If you suspect a forged receipt, do not proceed—escalate via Appeal immediately. AR Wallet's dispute team will investigate and rule in favor of the honest party.

Does AR Wallet offer phone support?

To maintain security and prevent impersonation scams, AR Wallet does not provide voice call support. All customer service is conducted through the official in-app live chat. Our support staff will never request your UPI PIN, SMS OTP, or password. Any call claiming to be from "AR Wallet support" is fraudulent.
